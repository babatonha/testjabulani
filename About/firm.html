About Us

Jabulani Private Expeditions is a tourism company which is there to make you happy on your holidays as the name says by itself, "Jabulani" which means happiness. It provides a full service pack from transport, food and accommodation for you to travel all over Southern Africa. The company was founded by Tafara Alfred, a man who was so passionate about tourism from a younger age. Our philosophy is to provide comfortable, more personal and interactive, holidays. Jabulani private Expeditions has positioned itself to be able to provide their customers with the product they require, whether it’s a budget or an upmarket holiday, catering for those who are looking for a value-for-money experience.

Our Vehicles

The company has got very beautiful vehicles and below are some of its features:

  1. Pop-up roofs for better game viewing
  2. Individual lockers
  3. Luggage compartment, which leaves the inside of the vehicle uncluttered but the vehicles have enough space for your hand luggage and camera bags inside the vehicle
  4. Electricity inside the vehicle allows cameras to be charged and fridge and freezer to run.
  5. PA system allowing for easy communication between the safari guide and our clients
  6. The 4×4 feature of our vehicle allows us to travel off the beaten track
  7. Large, slightly tinted windows for optimal game viewing, which protect you from the hot Africa sun.
  8. 12 forward facing, sizable passenger seats plus two crew seats fitted with seat belts promoting comfort and safety along your African journey
  9. Safety features: Air-helper suspension, seat belts, fire extinguisher, first aid kid & Netstar Tracking System
  10. MP3 stereo for some music along the way
