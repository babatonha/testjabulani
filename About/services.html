About Our Services

We here at Jabulani Private Tours are an inbound agency specializing in facilitating private, socially and economically responsible tourism in Zimbabwe, Botswana, Namibia, South Africa and all the other countries in Southern Africa. We take delight in ensuring that our clients get to experience the best of what Africa has to offer. Our goal is to make sure your expedition is as seamless as possible, and we fully leverage the collective experience, passion and commitment of our team to see to all your travel needs.

What we offer

The best planning and booking services covering all your needs, including:

  • Transport to anywhere within Southern Africa, from anywhere
  • Accommodation for the duration of your stay
  • The best Food and Beverage spots in the region
  • Tour guides to show you the way
  • Various forms of entertainment

Some of the Destinations to visit

Visit Zimbabwe

Visit the beautiful land of Zimbabwe. A journey to Zimbabwe will take you through an attractive patchwork of landscapes, from highveld, balancing boulders and flaming msasa trees, to laidback towns, lush mountains and lifeblood rivers.

Visit Botswana

Botswana is a land of stunning beauty, unimaginable vastness, isolation and worldliness as well as astoundingly prolific wildlife. Botswana’s principal tourist attractions are its game reserves, with hunting and photographic safaris available

Visit Namibia

Namibia is an arid, rough land full of contrasts, but still remains warm, inviting and easy to travel. As one of the most scarcely populated countries in the world, it’s not the right place to get lost in the crowds. What it is though is a top destination for those who enjoy nature.
