Southern Africa

The Southern African region is resplendant in natural beauty, warm and inviting people who truly understand the importance of hospitality, as espoused by he region's common philosophy of the spirit of Ubuntu.

Ubuntu / hunhu is a philosophy that defines humanity... the guideline to all human interaction. Regardless of where you find yourself in the region, across the diverse landscapes, languages and ethnicities, you will find that common thread that binds the peoples of the region under the same banner. It dictates that those who are our guests be treated as royalty or, better yet, family. Let us at Jabulani Private Expeditions be your guide to this remarkable region.
