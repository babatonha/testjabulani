Eastern Highlands

Home of Zimbabwe's Highest peaks

Resplendent with unmatched vistas of lush evergreen forests and towering vistas, the Eastern Highlands of Zimbabwe are a veritable promised-land for scientific discovery.

Overview

The Eastern Highlands are a series of mountainous areas near the border with Mozambique. These highlands stretch from Nyanga in the north where you will find the highest peak in Zimbabwe, Mount Nyangani, summitting proudly at 2593 metres. further south you'll encounter the breathtakingly lush Bvumba Mountains and the magnificent quartzite Chimanimani range, who's highest peak, Mt. Binga, straddles both Zimbabwe and Mozambique. The wide variety of endemic species of this trans-frontier park attract scientists and hikers from all over the world. Things to do

Visit the remarkable Mtarazi falls. Go on picturesque hikes and nature walks up the highlands, witnessing the lush evergreen forests and amazing vistas of world's view. Go fishing at the renowned TroutBeck Inn.
