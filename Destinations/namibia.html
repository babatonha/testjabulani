There are various destinations in Namibia and amongst them includes Etosha, Fish River Canyon and the Sossussvlei.

Etosha National Park

Etosha National Park is centered around the vast Etosha salt pan.

Sossuss Vlei

Sossusvlei is an enormous clay pan surrounded by gigantic, red sand dunes.
